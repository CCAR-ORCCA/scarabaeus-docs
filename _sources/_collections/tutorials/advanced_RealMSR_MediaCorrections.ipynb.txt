    "## 2. Tropospheric Delay Models\n",
    "\n",
    "Radio signals passing through Earth's troposphere acquire a **non-dispersive path-length delay**.\n",
    "\n",
    "SCB decomposes the zenith tropospheric delay into two additive components, each stored in a separate JSON file:\n",
    "\n",
    "| Component | File pattern | Model |\n",
    "|---|---|---|\n",

    "\n",
    "Both files are nested by site (`C10`, `C40`, `C60`) and wet/dry component.  `MediaCorrections.get_media_data_for_et()` retrieves the coefficient block that brackets any query epoch."

    "### 2.3 — Elevation mapping function\n",
    "\n",
    "The zenith delay applies only when the signal travels straight up.  For a real link at elevation angle $E$, the slant delay is:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\\delta_s = m(E) \\cdot \\delta_z$$\n",
    "\n",
    "SCB implements the **Chao (1972)** mapping function via `MediaCorrections.chao_mapping(elevation_rad)`, which returns separate dry and wet factors.  Both approach 1 at zenith (90°) and grow steeply near the horizon.\n",
    "\n",
    "> **Note:** Measurements below ~15° elevation are typically rejected in OD processing — the mapping function uncertainty and multipath contamination become unacceptably large."

    "---\n",
    "## 3. DSN Ramp Tables\n",
    "\n",
    "DSN uplink signals are not always transmitted at a constant frequency.  When a **frequency ramp** is in effect, the exciter follows a pre-programmed schedule stored in the tracking data as SFDU record type 9.\n",
    "\n",
    "| Field | Description |\n",
    "|---|---|\n",
    "| `sec` | Second-of-day at which the ramp interval begins |\n",
    "| `doy` | Day-of-year of the ramp interval |\n",
    "| `ramp_freq` | Exciter frequency at interval start [Hz] |\n",
    "| `ramp_rate` | Linear frequency rate during the interval [Hz/s] |\n",
    "| `ramp_type` | Integer flag identifying the ramp mode |\n",
    "\n",
    "`scb.RampTableManager.integrate(ramp_data, t1_sec, t3_sec, t1_doy, t3_doy)` integrates the piecewise-linear frequency profile between two epochs and returns the total accumulated phase — required to convert a sequential ranging observable to a time-of-flight when the uplink is ramped."
   ]
  },

    "### 3.2 — Integrated frequency sensitivity\n",
    "\n",
    "`RampTableManager.integrate()` is evaluated at 13 logarithmically spaced offsets from `t1`, spanning nanoseconds to thousands of seconds.  The result is scaled by the Range Unit (RU) conversion factor, giving accumulated phase in RU as a function of count interval.\n",
    "\n",
    "> **Key insight:** On a log-log scale, a slope of 1 means the uplink frequency is approximately constant over that interval — the ramp rate is negligible at that timescale."
   ]
  },
  {
   "cell_type": "code",
   "execution_count": null,
   "id": "16",
   "metadata": {},
   "outputs": [],
   "source": [
    "# Constants derived from the loaded ramp table\n",
    "# Skip ramp_type=0 entries — the integrator treats them as inactive intervals\n",
    "s9         = trk_data[\"aux_SFDU_9\"]\n",
    "valid_idx  = next(i for i, rt in enumerate(s9[\"ramp_type\"]) if rt != 0)\n",
    "RU         = 0.147530040053405          # Range Unit conversion factor (OREX X-band)\n",
    "t1_sec     = s9[\"sec\"][valid_idx]       # First active ramp epoch (sec of day)\n",
    "t1_doy     = int(s9[\"doy\"][valid_idx])\n",
    "t3_doy     = t1_doy\n",
    "delta_t    = 10 ** np.arange(-9, 4, dtype=float)   # 13 log-spaced offsets [s]\n",
    "\n",
    "f_int_RU = []\n",
    "for dt in delta_t:\n",
    "    f_int, _ = scb.RampTableManager.integrate(ramp_data, t1_sec, t1_sec + dt, t1_doy, t3_doy)\n",
    "    f_int_RU.append(RU * f_int)\n",
    "\n",
    "f_int_RU = np.array(f_int_RU)\n",

    "---\n",
    "## 4. RTLT Media Corrections for Sequential Ranging\n",
    "\n",
    "The zenith delay from Section 2 must be projected into the actual **round-trip light-time (RTLT)** geometry of a two-way pass.  The full pipeline is:\n",
    "\n",
    "| Step | Action |\n",
    "|---|---|\n",
    "| 1 | Load a tracking data file; identify the ground station |\n",
    "| 2 | Extract receive epochs `t3` from the sequential ranging observable |\n",
    "| 3 | Estimate transmit epochs `t1` via a two-step SPICE light-time computation |\n",
    "| 4 | Evaluate `computed_rtlt_delays()` at both `t1` (uplink) and `t3` (downlink) |\n",
    "| 5 | Sum the two one-way delays to get the total RTLT path-length correction |\n",
    "| 6 | Convert: meters → seconds → Range Units |\n",
    "\n",
    "The corrected delay is subtracted from the raw observable before it enters the OD filter."
   ]
  },

    "### 4.2 — Compute transmit epochs t1\n",
    "\n",
    "Sequential ranging observables are tagged at the **receive time** `t3`.  The transmit time `t1` is estimated in two SPICE steps:\n",
    "\n",
    "1. Compute the **downlink** one-way light time at `t3` (spacecraft → ground).\n",
    "2. Subtract from `t3` to approximate the spacecraft reception time; compute the **uplink** one-way light time (ground → spacecraft) at that epoch.\n",
    "3. `RTLT = t_uplink + t_downlink`,  `t1 = t3 − RTLT`."
   ]

    "### 4.4 — Compute RTLT tropospheric delays\n",
    "\n",
    "`computed_rtlt_delays()` evaluates the full model (seasonal + non-seasonal, dry + wet, Chao-mapped) at a given ET and returns the one-way path-length correction in **meters**.  The RTLT correction and unit chain are:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\\Delta p\\,[\\mathrm{m}] = \\Delta p(t_3) + \\Delta p(t_1)\n",
    "\\qquad\n",
    "\\Delta\\tau\\,[\\mathrm{s}] = \\frac{\\Delta p \\times 10^{-3}}{c}\n",
    "\\qquad\n",
    "\\Delta\\Phi\\,[\\mathrm{RU}] = \\Delta\\tau \\cdot f_\\mathrm{uplink}\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "where $c$ is the speed of light in km/s (`scb.constants.c`) and the factor $10^{-3}$ converts meters to kilometres."
   ]
  },
  {
   "cell_type": "code",
   "execution_count": null,
   "id": "25",

   "outputs": [],

    "SRA_tropo_t1 = []\n",
    "\n",
    "for ii in range(len(SRA_t3.times.values)):\n",
    "    t3 = SRA_t3.times.values[ii]\n",
    "    t1 = SRA_t1_epoch.times.values[ii]\n",
    "\n",
    "    p_t3 = media_correction.computed_rtlt_delays(query_time_et=t3, target_spice_id=target)\n",
    "    p_t1 = media_correction.computed_rtlt_delays(query_time_et=t1, target_spice_id=target)\n",
    "\n",
    "    SRA_tropo_t3.append(p_t3)\n",
    "    SRA_tropo_t1.append(p_t1)\n",
    "\n",
    "SRA_tropo_t3 = np.array(SRA_tropo_t3)\n",
    "SRA_tropo_t1 = np.array(SRA_tropo_t1)\n",
    "\n",
    "# RTLT path-length correction [m]\n",
    "SRA_delta_m    = SRA_tropo_t3 + SRA_tropo_t1\n",
