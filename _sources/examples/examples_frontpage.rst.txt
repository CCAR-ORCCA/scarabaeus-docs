========================
:gold:`Scarabaeus Examples`
========================
Examples and tutorials to teach and illustrate Scarabaeus and its applications. All tutorials are provided as Jupyter Notebooks.

Before beginning, users should have a basic understanding of Scarabaeus and its structure, see the :ref:`User Guide <user>`, as well as how ancillary data is used within the tutorials, see the :ref:`tutorials primer <tuts-setup>`.
